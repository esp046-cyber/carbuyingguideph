üìã Pre-Purchase Research

  • Set a realistic budget (30% down, 70% financing max)
  • Research model reliability and common issues
  • Check maintenance costs and parts availability
  • Read owner reviews and join FB groups
  • Compare insurance quotes from different providers

üí° Negotiation Tips

  • Visit multiple dealerships for best price
  • End of month/quarter = better deals
  • Ask for freebies: tint, mats, rust proofing
  • Negotiate total price, not monthly payment
  • Get pre-approved bank financing for leverage

üö® Red Flags (Used Cars)

  • Incomplete papers or "for transfer" tags
  • Mismatched VIN or chassis numbers
  • Fresh paint on old car (possible accident)
  • Seller rushing the sale
  • Unusually low price for year/model
  • No maintenance records available

üìÑ Required Documents

  • Valid driver's license
  • 2 valid IDs
  • Proof of income (payslip/ITR/bank statement)
  • Proof of billing (utilities under your name)
  • LTO registration (for trade-in)

üí° Maximize Resale Value

  • Choose popular colors: White, silver, black sell fastest
  • Keep complete records: All service receipts and OR/CR
  • Maintain religiously: Follow PMS schedule strictly
  • Avoid modifications: Stock cars sell better
  • Low mileage matters: Under 15,000 km/year is ideal
  • No accidents: Even repaired damage lowers value 20-30%
  • Clean title: First owner cars sell for 10-15% more

üìâ What Hurts Resale Value

  • Modified engine or suspension (-15-25%)
  • Aftermarket body kits (-10-20%)
  • Unusual colors like yellow, green (-10-15%)
  • High mileage (over 20,000 km/year) (-15-25%)
  • Smoking inside car (-5-10%)
  • Missing service records (-10-15%)
  • Flood damage history (-30-50%)

üïê Best Time to Sell

  • 3-5 years: Sweet spot - still reliable, good value
  • Before 100,000 km: Psychological barrier for buyers
  • December-January: Bonus season, more buyers
  • Before major repairs: Sell before expensive maintenance
  • Within warranty: Transferable warranty adds value
